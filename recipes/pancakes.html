Pancakes

Description

Delicious pancakes really easy to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 and half teaspoon sugar

Steps

  1. Use a bowl to mix all the ingredients.
  2. Whisk ingredients.
  3. Heat pan with a little oil.
  4. Add a little of the mix in the pan trying to do a circular shape.
  5. When there are bubbles visibles turn around the pancakes.
  6. Once it is ready, remove the pancake from the pan.
